▶1. Project Title & Description

Project Title: IT Networking Portfolio

This project delivers a portfolio of the expierence and the projects that were gained over the course of this academic career. This portfolio will have experience listed, the many projects that are being worked and have been worked on, and information in regards to the project holder.

▶2. Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Personal Mission Connection:

This project supports a personal mission as it puts a calling layer out there for different recruiters to see what a student may have in offer and what they are expecting towards graduation.

Vision Alignment:

This project shall put a future vision ahead of the project conductor as it can not only be used for networking in regards to recruiters and companies, but also consultants and other people out in this industry who may want to network. They can showcase their work, and recommend others as well.

Strategic Goal Support:

The strategic goals in which this project advances is that it has a good outlook for the future, and that once this portfolio is created, then forth it can be updated. It can also be chosen for different careers as well, as sometimes the demand of industries and economies ebb and flow.

▶3. Business Case & Justification

Professional Value:

This project will enhance professional value, as the networking opportunities and knowledge gained in regards to technology can only be a benefitting factor.

Personal Value:

A personal value that can be gained is a sense of accomplishment, as well as taking the first steps to put themselves out there to make career strides after undergraduate studies.

Learning Outcomes:

The learning outcomes that will be proceeded in this project is Project Management Skills, Technical/Web Design Skills, and Soft Skills.

▶4. High‑Level Scope

✅ In Scope for Semester Project

  • Updating content and providing weekly revisions to said website
  • Making sure the project meets business and professional requirements to present to recruiters
  • Merging all skills and projects within knowledge to make sure that this can be as effective as possible.
  • Managing the website using web-hosting services as needed.

🗺️ Roadmap Items (≤6 Months)

  • Make physical business cards to display the website available to improve opportunities
  • Making sure that the portfolio is within the project budget.
  • Making sure that the portfolio is reasonable with the variables of the project itself.

❌ Out of Scope

  • Making sure that the portfolio isn't too distracting and unprofessional
  • Don't drag out timeline expectations.

▶Project Methodology and Plan

Delivery Objective

The project will deliver a complete, launch-ready website. While the initial requirements are fairly predictive with a clear starting roadmap, later stages will benefit from an adaptive approach as priorities evolve.

Method Selection

Given the need for clarity up front and flexibility as work progresses, the project will use a Hybrid, Adaptive–Iterative approach. This supports structured planning while enabling regular feedback and course-correction.

Chosen Method: Adaptive–Iterative (Hybrid)

  • Why: Encourages constructive feedback, accommodates timeline shifts, and fits a week-by-week planner style.
  • What it looks like: Short iterations (sprints) with planned reviews; the website improves in visible increments.
  • Outcome: A complete site, refined through continuous feedback and iteration.

Approaches Considered

ApproachStrengthsLimitations / Fit
PredictiveClear, rigid structure; easy to plan early phases.Too inflexible as needs change; not the best fit overall.
