▶📝1. Project Title & Description

Project Title: Transition into Network Administration, Cybersecurity, or Network Operations

Similar to my Capstone course, in which the knowledge that was gained over my educational career, this project is in preparation for transitioning into a position of choosing either Cybersecurity, Network Operations, or Network Administration. Combining and showcasing different projects and experiences, along with certifications, will prepare me for the next steps after graduation.

▶🧭2. Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Personal Mission Connection:

To finish my final semester at CSULA, and to build projects and other tools that display my skillset to get an entry-level or mid-level opportunity in this field by the end of December 2025, or by May 2026.

Vision Alignment:

Successfully transitioning into a role in Network Administration, Network Operations, Cybersecurity, or any other technical field is where I aim to establish myself in a career that leverages my technical expertise.

Strategic Goal Support:

This project can only increase my chances of hireability, seeing with the amount of projects, past and present, along with internship experience that I currently have, which should make me an asset to the companies out there looking for Network, or Cybersecurity professionals.

▶💼3. Business Case & Justification

Professional Value:

This project will enhance professional value, as some of the job skills or technical knowledge are needed in some job descriptions. I want to meet those qualifications listed to at least have a chance for an interview.

Personal Value:

A personal value that can be gained is a sense of accomplishment, as well as taking the first steps to put themselves out there to make career strides after undergraduate studies. This project also highlights the expertise that I have gained.

Learning Outcomes:

The learning outcomes that will be covered in this project are Web Design Skills, as I have taken some courses before, and to gain and learn more Cybersecurity knowledge through more projects that I will showcase. Through projects, I will also learn proper documentation, and transferable skills ready for a career.

▶🛠️Project Methodology and Plan

Delivery Objective

The project will deliver a complete, launch-ready website. While the initial requirements are fairly predictive with a clear starting roadmap, later stages will benefit from an adaptive approach as priorities evolve.

Method Selection

Given the need for clarity up front and flexibility as work progresses, the project will use a Hybrid, Adaptive–Iterative approach. This supports structured planning while enabling regular feedback and course-correction.

Chosen Method: Adaptive–Iterative (Hybrid)

  • Why: Encourages constructive feedback, accommodates timeline shifts, and fits a week-by-week planner style.
  • What it looks like: Short iterations (sprints) with planned reviews; the website improves in visible increments.
  • Outcome: A complete site, refined through continuous feedback and iteration.

▶🧰11. Project Tools

Step 1: Tools Explored

Based on some of my semester projects that I have designed before, I have used some of these tools in a similar shape and fashion. These tools would be Trello, Microsoft Project, and Excel/Google Sheets.

Chosen Tool: Trello — I am familiar with a kanban board, which allows me to continuously post updates, refer to what I have set up already, and plan what is coming up in the future.

Tool Justification

  • Board markings make it easier to see which weeks are coming up..
  • Clear visibility of tasks that are finished or in progress.
  • User‑friendly with minimal learning curve.
  • School subscription already available; supports collaboration and sharing progress.

Step 2: Structure Overview

My project is set up in sprints (Sprint 1–5/6), broken into separate parts to space out work. Trello’s structure supports adaptive changes, making it easier to track progress. Having used it before, I find it straightforward — most labels and sections are predefined and just need updating.
